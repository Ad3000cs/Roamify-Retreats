The Northern Paradise

The Northern India is famous for its scenic mountains, lakes, historical mountains and forts. It is also famous for its delicious food. We offer two exquisite packages to the best locations in North India.

Package 1

Total Days - 14

Price - $ 5,500 (excluding car charges)

Amritsar - 3 days

  • Stay at the luxurious Taj Swarna.
  • Enjoy the best local food at Kesar Da Dhaba - more than 100 years old and many more!
  • See the spectacular Golden Temple
  • Revisit the history and know about the struggle for independence of India at Jallianwala Bagh.
  • Shop at famous colorful and vibrant local markets.

Delhi - 5 days

  • Stay at the Leela Palace
  • Explore the rich history of India in its capital. See various world famous monuments like the India Gate, Red fort etc. See the spectacular sound and light shows at each of the monuments you visit.
  • Enjoy the local street food, explore the rich culture, shop at world famous local markets at Chandni Chawk , Dilli Haat and more!

Lucknow - 3 days

  • Stay at the Holiday Inn
  • Who doesn’t want to savor the world famous Tunde Kebab and Biryani?
  • See historical monuments like Bara Imambara, Dilkusha Kothi Palace and much more!

Agra - 3 days

  • Stay at The Oberoi Amarvilas. Just 600 meters away from Taj Mahal!
  • See one of the Marvels of the world The impeccable Taj Mahal.
  • See more historical places like the Akbar’s tomb and more!
  • Well, every place you go in India, the food is always unique and special!

Package 2

Total days - 11

Price = $4,000 (excluding car charges)

Manali - 3 days

  • Stay at Span resort and spa.
  • Enjoy the lovely nature and hills!
  • A great place to have a nice Bonfire

Dehradun - 5 days

  • Stay at the Six Senses Vana - A wellness Retreat
  • Great place to be around with nature.
  • Stay offers wellness programs, a detachment from the fast moving and stressful lives.
  • Explore the Forest Research Institute, Kalinga War memorial and more!

Mussoorie - 3 days

  • Stay at JW Marriot Mussoorie Walnut Grove Resort and Spa
  • Enjoy the beautiful views of the mountains and the town amidst the clouds!
  • Great place to indulge in long treks and a bonfire.
