The Western Cultural and Heritage Hub

These packages cover mainly two states, Package 1: Rajasthan - World famous for rich culture, heritage and architectural marvels and the Thar Desert. Package 2: Gujrat - Very famous for Rann of Kutch - one of the world's largest white deserts and Gir national park, the only home to Asiatic Lions and more!.

Package 1

Total days - 18

Price - $7,700 (excluding car charges)

Jaipur - 6 days

  • Stay at The Taj Jai Mahal Palace - A luxurious heritage property.
  • The Jai Mahal Palace was constructed in 1745. It is one of the most stunning piece of the rich Rajasthan heritage. It was converted into a luxurious five star hotel by Taj.
  • See the best of India’s rich Architecture - Hawa Mahal, Jal Mahal, Nahargarh Fort and much more!
  • Shop at the best of colorful and vibrant markets like Tripolia Bazaar, Johari Bazaar and more!
  • Best place to shop colorful clothes, hand made paintings, designer carpets etc.
  • Famous for its street food - Pyaaz Ki Kachori, Rajasthani Thali and much more!

Udaipur - 5 days

  • Stay at the stunning 250 year old lake palace converted to a marvelous hotel by Taj - Taj Lake Palace .
  • Udaipur is another chapter in Rajasthan’s rich culture and history.
  • A full day trip to the Kumbhalgarh Fort - The Great Wall Of India, the second longest wall of the world.
  • See the Udaipur City palace, Maharana Pratap Memorial and much more!
  • Just like Jaipur, Udaipur is also famous for its rich culture and vibrant markets.

Jaisalmer - 4 days

  • Stay at the Jaisalmer Marriot Resort and Spa.
  • Every place in Rajasthan has its own history and heritage.
  • Enjoy Camel ride in the Thar Desert.
  • See the Jaisalmer Fort, Jaisalmer War museum and more!

Jodhpur - 3 days

  • Stay at the Umaid Bhavan Palace
  • See Mehrangarh fort, Umaid Bhavan Palace Museum and more!

Package 2

Total days - 15

Price - $7,000 (excluding car charges)

Ahmedabad - 3 days

  • Stay at Courtyard By Marriot
  • See the world’s tallest statue - The Statue of Unity
  • A tour of the world’s largest cricket stadium - The Narendra Modi Stadium.
  • A tour of Sabarmati Gandhi Ashram - The ashram (home) of the Father of India a world-renowned Freedom Fighter and an icon for peace - Mahatma Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi a.k.a. Mahatma Gandhi
  • Many more places of Interest!!

Gir - 7 days

  • Stay at Woods at Sasan
  • Enjoy the ancient practices of Ayurveda and Yoga for 5 days offered by the stay.
  • A full day trip to Gir National Park, the only home to Asiatic Lions.

Kutch - 3 days

  • Stay at Time Square Club, Resort and Spa
  • Visit the ruins of Dholavira, a UNESCO world heritage site of the oldest and ancient Indus Valley Civilization City.
  • Enjoy the white Desert safari in Rann of Kutch

Surat - 2 days

  • Stay at Park Inn Surat by Radisson
  • Best place to buy the highest quality in the world of silk products and diamond jewellery.
