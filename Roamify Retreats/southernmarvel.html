The Southern Marvel

South India is famous for its intricately designed ancient temples, food and culture. It also has relaxing beaches, huge waterfalls and the world's best spice plantations.

Package 1

Total days - 12

Price - $6,500 (excluding car charges)

Bengalore - 3 days

  • Stay at the Leela Bhartiya City Hotel
  • See the world renowned ISKCON Temple in Bengalore
  • Techies and Science addicts Alert!! - see the HAL heritage centre and museum, the tech park and more!
  • Shop at Orion Mall, one of the biggest malls in the city

Kochi - 3 days

  • Stay at The Le Meridian
  • Day trip to Athirapally waterfalls - The largest water fall of Kerela
  • Enjoy boating in the Back waters of Kerela

Munnar - 3 days

  • Stay amidst the clouds at Chandys Drizzle Drops
  • See how Tea is made and grown in plantations at TATA tea factory
  • A day visit to arguably the best spice farms in the world in Thekkady

Trivandrum/Kovalum - 3 days

  • Stay in a villa at traditional South Indian property - Ama Stays and Trails in Trivandrum
  • Day trip to wonderful beaches in Kovalum.
  • Enjoy the tastes of South India with the best seafood, various other South Indian dishes, and Sadya - the traditional meal of South India.
