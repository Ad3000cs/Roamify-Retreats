North East Adventure

The North East of India is known for is rich natural beauty. every place you visit here will have the best quality air, dense forests with the sound of crickets and the natural smell of mother Earth. This trip will be a big gift to your body as well as your mind.

Package 1

Total days - 9

Price - $3,500 (excluding car charges)

Guwahati - 3 days

  • Stay at the Radisson Blu Guwahati
  • Scenic view of the mighty Brahmaputra river just 8 km from the property!
  • Devour the best of northeast food - Momos, Thukpa and more!
  • North east is a bird watcher’s paradise! Keep the binoculars and professional cameras ready for the Deepor Beel Bird Santuary.

Shillong - 4 days

  • Stay in a luxurious cottage at Ri Kynjai Serenity by the lake resort.
  • Enjoy boating at the Umiam lake
  • Experience the beautiful views of the city from Shillong Point.
  • Also see the amazing Elephant waterfall and walk through the enchanting sacred forests.
  • A day’s trip to Mawlynnong - The cleanest village in Asia.

Kaziranga - 2 days

  • Stay in the middle of the world famous Tea estates of Assam at Kaziranga Golf Resort.
  • A day trip to the UNESCO World Heritage Site - Kaziranga National Park.
  • The national park is home to two thirds of the world’s Indian Rhinos, many species of birds, tigers and many more animals!

Package 2

Total days - 8

Price - $3,000 (excluding car charges)

Kaziranga - 2 days

  • Stay in the middle of the world famous Tea estates of Assam at Kaziranga Golf Resort.
  • A day trip to the UNESCO World Heritage Site - Kaziranga National Park.
  • The national park is home to two thirds of the world’s Indian Rhinos, many species of birds, tigers and many more animals!

Itanagar/Ziro Valley - 3 days

  • Stay at Cygnett Inn Trendz, Itanagar
  • Detoxify yourself at Ziro with fresh air and mystic vibes.
  • Experience the World renowned International Music Festival in the beautiful Ziro Valley.
  • Wine lovers Alert! - see how fruit wines are made at Nara Abba Winery.

Tawang - 3 days

  • Stay at Timilo Boutique
  • See the Tawang Monastry - The largest monastery of India and second largest in the world.
  • Explore the snowbound Sela Pass at 13,700 ft!
  • See the Bumla Pass - the famous India - China Border and the Tawang War memorial - dedicated to the War heroes of the Indian Army who fought the 1962 India China War.
